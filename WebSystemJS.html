Different Types of Dogs

Labrador Retriever

Labradors are friendly, intelligent, and one of the most popular dog breeds worldwide.

German Shepherd

German Shepherds are loyal, confident, and often used as police or service dogs.

Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers are gentle, loving, and great family pets known for their golden coats.

Bulldog

Bulldogs are calm, courageous, and affectionate with a distinctive wrinkled face.

Chihuahua

Chihuahuas are tiny, lively, and known for their bold personalities despite their small size.

Poodle

Poodles are intelligent, active, and elegant dogs, often seen with curly coats and known for their trainability.
